Master Mind

Rules of the game :

You need to guess the right combination of 4 colors in the right order.
Pick one color in the pick column, it will be added to the combination in the choose column.
When your combination is completed, the check column will display if your choice fits the combination !
A will appear if you selected the right color at the correct place
A if you selected the right color but at the incorrect place !
